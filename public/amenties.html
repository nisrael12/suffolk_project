Amenities

Dine with Us

Eat freshly prepared food from our garden and local farmers. Serving three meals
a day until 11:00pm.

Drink with Us

Sample local beers and wines, as well as inventive cocktails, plus complimentary iced
coffee on tap all day.

Ride with Us

Enjoy free rental bikes during your stay. We'd be happy to point you toward some of
our favorite trails, shopping and restaurants, all accessible on two wheels.

Focus on Yourself

We have a network of excellent massage therapists and other bodywork
professionals and would be happy to arrange an in-room treatment during your stay.

Read Widely

Our library boasts over a thousand titles on a variety of subjects. Check out as many
as you’d like, as often as you’d like.

Bring a Friend

We are proud to be a dog-friendly property and would be happy to accommodate
your pet for a small additional fee. Please inquire when you book your stay for more
information.
